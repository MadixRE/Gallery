Edward Snowden

Topic: Global Surveillance & Digital Privacy

In 2013, Edward Snowden, a former NSA contractor, leaked classified information revealing the extent of global surveillance programs. His actions sparked a massive international debate about the balance between national security and individual privacy.

The Impact

Snowden's revelations led to significant changes in how technology companies handle user data and increased the adoption of end-to-end encryption across the internet.
