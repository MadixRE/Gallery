Marcus Hutchins

Topic: The WannaCry Kill-Switch

Known online as "MalwareTech," Marcus Hutchins became a global hero in 2017 when he discovered a "kill-switch" in the code of the WannaCry ransomware, effectively stopping an attack that was crippling hospitals and businesses worldwide.

His story is a powerful narrative of the "reformed hacker," showing how technical skills can be redirected from mischief toward global protection.
