Mr.Robot

Mr. Robot is a techno-thriller series that follows Elliot Alderson, a brilliant but highly unstable cybersecurity engineer and vigilante hacker. He is recruited by a mysterious anarchist known as "Mr. Robot" to join fsociety, a hacktivist group aiming to cancel all consumer debt by taking down one of the world's largest conglomerates, E Corp.
