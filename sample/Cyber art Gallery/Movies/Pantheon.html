Pantheon

Pantheon is a high-concept, animated sci-fi drama that stands out for its "hard sci-fi" approach to Uploaded Intelligence (UI). The story follows Maddie, a bullied teenager who begins receiving mysterious messages from her deceased father, David. It turns out David’s consciousness wasn't just stored; it was "uploaded" into the cloud after an experimental brain-scanning procedure. ​As Maddie reconnects with this digital version of her father, she discovers a massive global conspiracy involving tech giants competing to master UI technology, which threatens to trigger a new kind of world war.
